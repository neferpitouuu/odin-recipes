Chicken tagine

Description:

Tagine is a classic North African stew made with meats and vegetables braised in a gently spiced sauce.

There are many varieties of tagines. Meat, fish and all manner of vegetables can make their way into this stew.

However,chicken-tagine is considered one of the best types of tagines ever, and you probably don't want to miss this one. So what are we waiting for! let's make one!

Ingredients:

  • Bone-in skin-on chicken thighs
  • Ras el hanout
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Dried apricots
  • Chickpeas
  • Garlic and onion
  • Canned tomato
  • Chicken stock
  • Coriander / cilantro
  • Preserved lemon

Steps:

  1. Cover the dried apricots with boiling water then leave for 30 minutes to plump up.
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper and brown the skin really well in a pan.
  3. Saut√© the onion and garlic, then cook off the spices briefly.
  4. Add the chickpeas, apricot, tomato, preserved lemon, stock and cinnamon stick. Stir, then bring it to a simmer
  5. Just place the chicken on top, skin side up.
  6. Adjust the heat as needed so it's simmering gently
  7. Remove the lid then simmer uncovered for 20 minutes
  8. Serving!
